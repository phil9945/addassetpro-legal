Privacy Policy – Add Asset Pro

Last updated: 2025-11-11

This Privacy Policy describes how Add Asset Pro (the “App”) handles your information. The App is developed and published by Philip Mony (“I”, “me”, or “the developer”), based in British Columbia, Canada.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

Add Asset Pro is an offline utility app designed to help you capture and manage asset information using your device. The App stores data locally on your device and does not send your data to any external server controlled by the developer.

The App is supplied “as is”, without any warranties, guarantees, or official affiliation with any company or organization.

2. Data the App May Handle

The App allows you to enter and store information such as (but not limited to):

  • Asset details (e.g., description, condition, purchase price, model/type, serial number, barcode)
  • Session information (e.g., file prefixes, scan locations)
  • Optional device-related context (if enabled in settings), such as:
    • Date last inventoried
    • Device used (e.g., iPhone / iPad)
    • Device user name

This information is entered or generated by you and is stored locally on your device in CSV and/or PDF files.

3. Location and GPS Data

If enabled in the configuration screen, the App may include the following in your CSV output:

  • Approximate latitude
  • Approximate longitude

These values are included only in the CSV files saved on your device and are used solely for your own asset tracking purposes. The App does not transmit this location data to the developer or any external server.

You can turn GPS-related options on or off at any time in the configuration screen.

4. Local Storage Only

All asset data, configuration choices, and generated CSV/PDF files are stored locally on your device in the App’s document storage area.

The App does not:

  • create an online account,
  • upload your asset data to a remote server, or
  • sync your data via a custom cloud service.

Any sharing of CSV/PDF files happens only when you choose to export or share them using standard iOS/iPadOS share options (e.g., Mail, Files, AirDrop, or third-party apps).

Because the App does not use a developer-controlled server, you remain fully in control of your data.

5. No Analytics, No Tracking, No Ads

Add Asset Pro:

  • does not include third-party advertising SDKs,
  • does not collect analytics or tracking identifiers, and
  • does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

The developer does not receive any information about how you use the App, other than what you may choose to share directly (for example, by emailing a question or support request).

6. How You Can Manage or Delete Your Data

Because all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete individual CSV/PDF files from the “Saved Sessions” area or from the Files app (if exported there).
  • You can clear session data using the in-app options.
  • You can delete the App from your device at any time, which will also remove the App’s local data stored in its container on that device.

If you have exported files (for example, to iCloud Drive, local Files, email, or other apps), those copies must be deleted manually from those locations.

7. Children’s Privacy

Add Asset Pro is a general-purpose utility application and is not specifically directed at children. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data is entered and controlled by the user of the device.

8. Third-Party Services

The App relies on Apple’s iOS/iPadOS frameworks (such as camera, filesystem, and location services where enabled) to function. These components are provided by Apple and are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies and terms.

The developer does not integrate separate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

9. Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, its security depends largely on:

  • your device’s operating system (iOS/iPadOS), and
  • any security features you enable, such as device passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, and encrypted backups.

The App does not implement its own encryption layer on top of what the operating system provides.

10. International Users

The App is developed and published in British Columbia, Canada. Regardless of where you are located, the App does not transmit your asset data to a developer-controlled server. Any files you export or share may be stored by services you choose (such as iCloud, email providers, or cloud storage), which are governed by their own privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the App’s functionality or in applicable laws or regulations.

When the Policy is updated, the “Last updated” date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the App after changes are published will constitute your acceptance of the updated Policy.

12. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how the App handles data, please use the contact information provided on the App Store listing for Add Asset Pro to reach the developer, Philip Mony.
